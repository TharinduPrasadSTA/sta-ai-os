# Team Directory

> This file maps every person you work with across your business(es). The AI OS uses this for relationship tracking, delegation intelligence, meeting context, and communication management.

---

## How This Connects

- **personal-info.md** defines your role and responsibilities
- **companies.md** describes the businesses these people work in
- **This file** maps who does what and how often you should be in touch
- **strategy.md** helps the AI OS suggest delegation opportunities
- **current-data.md** can track team performance metrics
- **integrations.md** lists the tools your team uses

---

## Why This Matters

As a founder, your relationships are your leverage. The AI OS should know who's on your team, what they're responsible for, and when you're falling behind on communication. It should flag when you haven't spoken to a key person in too long, suggest delegation opportunities, and provide context about people before meetings.
