# Personal Info

> This file captures who you are, how you think, and what you optimise for. It is the most critical context file in your AI OS — without it, the system is just a generic tool. With it, the system thinks like you.

---

## How This Connects

- **This file** defines how you think and what drives you
- **companies.md** describes your business(es)
- **team.md** maps your people
- **strategy.md** outlines where you're going
- **current-data.md** shows where you actually are
- **integrations.md** lists your connected data sources

---

## Why This Matters

Your AI OS needs to make decisions the way you would. It needs to understand your priorities, your non-negotiables, and your instincts. Without this context, every recommendation is generic. With it, recommendations feel like they came from someone who genuinely understands your business.

This is the one file no one else can write for you. Take the time to get it right.
